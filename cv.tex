\section{Research}
I am interested in both theory and applications of machine learning.
In my PhD, I have been working on various areas in probabilistic machine learning including Gaussian processes, variational inference, Bayesian optimization, and active learning.
Before starting my PhD, I worked on generative modeling, convex optimization, and adversarial robustness of deep learning models.
